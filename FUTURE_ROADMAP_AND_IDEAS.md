## üéÅ 1. Reward System Alternatives (Post-Tango Card)

If automated gift cards are off the table, we need compelling alternatives to drive user engagement.

### A. The "Manual Concierge" Model (Short Term)
*   **How it works:** Users redeem points for specific items (Gift Cards, Merch, Digital Codes). You receive an email and fulfill it manually.
*   **Pros:** Zero tech integration, personal touch, high flexibility.
*   **Cons:** Doesn't scale beyond ~100 active redeemers.
*   **Verdict:** Best for immediate launch.

### B. "GG Loop Exclusive" Digital Goods (High Margin)
*   **Profile Cosmetics:** Animated avatars, profile borders, "Founder" badges, glowing username effects.
*   **Discord Perks:** Special roles, private channel access, priority support.
*   **Tournament Entry:** Use points to buy-in to cash-prize tournaments (legal review required).
*   **Coaching:** Redeem points for 1-on-1 coaching sessions with high-rank players (partner program).

### C. Crypto / Web3 Integration (Long Term)
*   **Tokenization:** Convert GG Coins to a blockchain token (e.g., on Solana or Polygon for low fees).
*   **NFT Trophies:** Mint achievements as soulbound NFTs.
*   **Pros:** High hype factor, automated value transfer.
*   **Cons:** High barrier to entry for non-crypto users, regulatory complexity.

## üéÆ 2. Platform Expansion Ideas

### A. "Squads" & Team Features
*   **Clan System:** Users form "Squads" (Guilds).
*   **Squad Leaderboards:** Aggregate points of all members.
*   **Squad Challenges:** "Win 50 games as a squad this week."
*   **Benefit:** Increases retention through peer pressure and social bonding.

### B. Tournament Engine
*   **Automated Brackets:** Users sign up, system generates brackets.
*   **Result Verification:** Use Riot API to verify match results automatically.
*   **Entry Fees:** Points or Cash (Stripe/PayPal).
*   **Prizes:** Cash pool or massive point jackpots.

### C. Content Creator Partner Program
*   **Streamer Dashboard:** Streamers get a unique referral link.
*   **Overlay Widget:** "Current GG Loop Rank" widget for their OBS.
*   **Audience Challenges:** "Beat [Streamer]'s score" challenges.

### A. Riot API Key Rotation System
*   **Problem:** Keys expire.
*   **Solution:** Build a service that alerts admins via Discord/Email 3 days before expiration, or automates renewal if Riot offers an enterprise API.

### B. Mobile App (PWA or Native)
*   **Current:** Responsive web.
*   **Future:** React Native app. Push notifications for "Your match is synced!" or "Daily streak about to expire!" are huge retention drivers.

### C. AI Coach Integration
*   **Concept:** Analyze match data (KDA, CS/min, vision score) and use an LLM to generate personalized improvement tips.
*   **Hook:** "Based on your last 5 Jinx games, you're dying too much in the first 10 mins. Try playing safer until level 6."
