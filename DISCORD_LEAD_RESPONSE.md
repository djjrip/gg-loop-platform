## Questions to Ask (Discovery)

1. **Product Details:**
   - What's your platform called?
   - What games do you support?
   - What's your verification method?

2. **Stage:**
   - Are you live or still building?
   - How many users do you have?
   - Funded or bootstrapped?

3. **Collaboration Ideas:**
   - Integration (cross-platform rewards)?
   - White-label (you power their rewards)?
   - Co-marketing (promote each other)?
   - Acquisition (are they selling)?

4. **Timeline:**
   - When did you start building?
   - When do you plan to launch (if not live)?
   - What's your growth strategy?

## Template Selection Guide

**Use Long Response if:**
- Professional tone needed
- Want to establish credibility
- First impression matters

**Use Short Response if:**
- Casual conversation
- Speed is priority
- They seem like a peer/friend

**Use Competitor Response if:**
- You suspect they're building same thing
- Want to stay open but cautious
- Unsure of their intentions
