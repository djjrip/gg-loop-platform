## HOW PAYMENT WORKS

### Step 1: User Signs Up via Your Link
- User clicks your referral link
- Signs up for GG LOOP (free)
- Gets tagged as your referral in database

### Step 2: User Subscribes (Optional)
- User subscribes to Bronze ($4.99), Pro ($9.99), or Elite ($19.99)
- Subscription revenue tracked to your account

### Step 3: You Earn Revenue Share
- **Example:** User subscribes to Pro ($9.99/month)
- **Your Cut (20%):** $2.00/month
- **Your Cut (30%):** $3.00/month

### Step 4: Monthly Payout
- End of month: Calculate total earnings
- If above minimum payout: Send payment
- If below minimum: Roll over to next month

## EXAMPLE EARNINGS (DETAILED)

### Example 1: Gaming Creator (10k TikTok followers)
**Month 1:**
- 100 signups via referral link
- 10 subscribe to Bronze ($4.99/month)
- Your cut (20%): $0.99 x 10 = $9.90
- **Payout:** $0 (below $25 minimum, rolls over)

**Month 2:**
- 50 new signups
- 5 more subscribe to Bronze
- 2 subscribe to Pro ($9.99/month)
- Your cut: ($0.99 x 15) + ($2.00 x 2) = $18.85
- **Total:** $9.90 + $18.85 = $28.75
- **Payout:** $28.75 ✅

**Month 3:**
- 25 new signups
- 3 more subscribe to Pro
- Your cut: ($0.99 x 15) + ($2.00 x 5) = $24.85
- **Payout:** $24.85 (below minimum, rolls over)

**Month 4:**
- 10 new signups
- 1 subscribes to Elite ($19.99/month)
- Your cut: ($0.99 x 15) + ($2.00 x 5) + ($4.00 x 1) = $28.85
- **Total:** $24.85 + $28.85 = $53.70
- **Payout:** $53.70 ✅

**Annual Total:** ~$300-500/year passive

### What Happens When Users Cancel?
- User cancels subscription → You stop earning from that user

### Retention Incentives
- **Month 3 Bonus:** +10% if 80%+ of your referrals still subscribed
- **Month 6 Bonus:** +20% if 70%+ still subscribed
- **Annual Bonus:** +50% if 60%+ still subscribed after 12 months
