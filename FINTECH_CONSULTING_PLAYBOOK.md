## Discovery Call Script

**Opening:**
"Thanks for the call. I help fintech companies solve payment operations issues. Before we dive in, tell me: what's your current payment setup?"

**Questions to Ask:**

**Integration:**
1. Which payment processor(s) are you using?
2. How are you handling webhooks?
3. What's your retry logic for failed payments?

**Reconciliation:**
4. How do you reconcile transactions daily?
5. Manual process or automated?
6. How long does end-of-day reconciliation take?

**Compliance:**
7. Have you had a payment audit yet?
8. Where are you storing transaction logs?
9. What's your data retention policy?

**Pain Points:**
10. What payment issue keeps you up at night?
11. What takes the most time in your ops workflow?
12. If you could automate one thing, what would it be?
