## WHEN X HAPPENS, DO Y (DECISION RULES)

### High Clicks + High Conversions + High Revenue
**What it means:** WINNER - This is working  
**What to do:**
- Make 2-3 more pieces of content on this topic
- Test different angles (review, tutorial, comparison)
- Promote more aggressively (pin comment, bio link)
- Track what made it successful (hook, CTA, timing)

### High Clicks + Low Conversions + Low Revenue
**What it means:** Good content, bad offer OR wrong audience  
**What to do:**
- Check if offer is competitive (price, value prop)
- Test different CTA (more direct, more context)
- Try different platform (TikTok ‚Üí Discord or vice versa)
- If still low after 2 weeks, rotate out

### Low Clicks + High Conversions + High Revenue
**What it means:** Good offer, bad visibility  
**What to do:**
- Improve content hook (first 3 seconds)
- Post at better time (6-9pm PST)
- Use trending sounds/formats (TikTok)
- Pin comment with link
- Update bio link

### Low Clicks + Low Conversions + Low Revenue
**What it means:** LOSER - Kill it  
**What to do:**
- Stop making content on this topic
- Remove from bio link
- Archive program in `IMPACT_PROGRAMS_DB.json`
- Move on to next program

### High Clicks + No Conversions for 2+ Weeks
**What it means:** Broken link OR checkout issue  
**What to do:**
- Test link yourself (click and try to buy)
- Check Impact.com dashboard (is program still active?)
- Contact brand/Impact support
- Replace with backup program
