- [ ] Read full TOS and developer agreement
- [ ] No "commercial use prohibited" clause

### 4. Competitor Analysis
- [ ] At least 3 similar platforms exist without legal issues
- [ ] No recent cease-and-desist letters to competitors
- [ ] Similar services operating for 2+ years successfully

### 5. Legal Review
- [ ] Gaming lawyer reviewed integration ($500-2K)
- [ ] Written legal opinion obtained
- [ ] Risk assessment: LOW
- [ ] Contingency plan if TOS changes

### 6. Final Approval
- [ ] Founder approval
- [ ] Legal counsel approval
- [ ] Risk < 5% of legal action
