## Potentially Misleading Copy

### 1. "Get Paid to Play" Messaging

| Current | Problem | Proposed Replacement |
|---------|---------|----------------------|
| "Earn rewards for playing" | Implies automatic rewards | "Earn points toward rewards by playing verified matches" |
| "Get paid to play games" | Implies cash | "Turn your gameplay into points, then into rewards" |

### 2. Points vs Real Rewards Clarity

| Current | Problem | Proposed Replacement |
|---------|---------|----------------------|
| Points shown as currency | Confusing value | Add disclaimer: "Points are not currency and have no cash value" |
| Reward redemption implied instant | May be manual | Add: "Rewards processed within 48 hours" |

### 3. Verification Constraints

| Missing Disclosure | Required Addition |
|--------------------|-------------------|
| Manual verification phase | "During validation, upgrades are manual within 24 hours" |
| Match verification requirement | "Points awarded for verified gameplay only" |
| Anti-cheat review | "Unusual activity is reviewed. Fraudulent claims are removed." |

---

## Transparency Locks (NEVER REMOVE)

These statements must always be present somewhere in user-facing flows:

1. **"Points are not currency and have no cash value."**
2. **"Rewards require verified gameplay."**
3. **"During validation phase, upgrades are processed manually within 24 hours."**
4. **"Suspicious activity is reviewed. Cheaters are removed."**
5. **"GG LOOP is not affiliated with game publishers."**
