## BRAND VOICE

### Tone Attributes
1. **Casual:** "This is how I do it" not "Here's how to do it"
2. **Honest:** "Yeah it's affiliate but it works" not "Amazing opportunity!"
3. **Direct:** "Link in bio" not "Check out the link in my bio for more information"
4. **Real:** "I'm broke but optimizing" not "I'm a successful entrepreneur"
5. **Cultural:** "For the culture" not "For the community"

### What We Say
- ✅ "This is what I actually use"
- ✅ "Broke gamer investing 101"
- ✅ "How I flew to Manila for $287"
- ✅ "Ranked sweats: this is your sign"
- ✅ "For the culture"
- ✅ "No cap"
- ✅ "This is the move"

### What We DON'T Say
- ❌ "You NEED this NOW"
- ❌ "This will change your life"
- ❌ "Limited time offer" (unless actually true)
- ❌ "Get rich quick"
- ❌ "Guaranteed results"
- ❌ "Revolutionary platform"
- ❌ "Game-changing opportunity"

## CONTENT VOICE

### TikTok/Instagram
**Style:** Fast, punchy, relatable  
**Length:** 15-60 seconds  
**Hook:** First 3 seconds make or break  
**Example:**
```
"This is how I fixed my ping and climbed to Diamond"
[Show before/after]
"Link in bio if you're tired of blaming lag"
```

### Discord
**Style:** Conversational, helpful, community-first  
**Length:** 2-5 sentences  
**Tone:** Friend helping friend  
**Example:**
```
Ranked players: stop blaming your internet

GearUP Booster → [link]

Reduces lag for ranked grind. Tested it myself, actually works.

(Affiliate link - I earn a small commission if you try it)
```

### Website
**Style:** Clear, direct, benefit-focused  
**Length:** Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)  
**Tone:** Professional but not corporate  
**Example:**
```
Earn rewards for playing the games you already love.

Track your stats, climb the leaderboards, redeem points for real rewards. No crypto, no NFTs, just gaming.

Join 2,000+ gamers already earning.
```

### Email
**Style:** Personal, value-first, no spam  
**Length:** 100-200 words  
**Tone:** CEO talking to community  
**Example:**
```
Hey [Name],

Quick update: PayPal subscriptions are now live.

Bronze ($4.99), Pro ($9.99), Elite ($19.99). All tiers get monthly points + exclusive rewards.

Not pushing you to subscribe - just wanted you to know it's an option now.

- Jayson
```

## VISUAL STYLE

### Photography
- **Real:** Actual setup photos, not stock images
- **Lighting:** Natural or warm, not harsh
- **Composition:** Clean but not sterile

### Graphics
- **Style:** Modern, clean, minimal
- **Colors:** GG Blue + GG Purple primary
- **Icons:** Simple, recognizable
- **Avoid:** Clipart, cheesy graphics, outdated styles

### Video
- **Format:** Vertical (9:16) for TikTok/Instagram
- **Length:** 15-60 seconds (sweet spot: 30-45)
- **Editing:** Fast cuts, text overlays, trending sounds
- **Quality:** iPhone quality is fine, don't need cinema camera

## BRAND RULES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

### Rule 1: Always Disclose Affiliate Links
- Every affiliate link must include disclosure
- "Affiliate link" or "I earn a commission" minimum
- Transparency > conversions

### Rule 2: Never Overpromise
- Manual fulfillment = honest about timelines
- "Rewards take 7-14 days" not "Instant rewards"
- Under-promise, over-deliver

### Rule 3: Community First
- Discord is the heart of GG LOOP
- Respond to questions, help users
- No ghosting the community

### Rule 4: No Fake Urgency
- Don't use "ONLY 2 LEFT" unless actually true
- Don't create false scarcity
- Real deals only

### Rule 5: Respect the Culture
- Filipino references must be authentic
- Basketball/sneaker content must be real
- Don't appropriate or fake it

1. Is this honest?
2. Does this add value?
3. Does this feel like GG LOOP?
