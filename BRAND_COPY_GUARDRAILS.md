### 1. NO FABRICATED NUMBERS, EVER.
*   **PROHIBITED:** Inventing user counts (e.g., "2,000+ gamers"), revenue, conversion rates, specific reward quantities ("Top 50"), or specific dollar values without hard data.
*   **REQUIRED:** If you don't have a CEO-confirmed number, use placeholders: `[X players]`, `[size TBD]`, `[amount TBD]`, or generic terms like "early-stage", "growing community".

### 2. NO PROMISING REWARDS YOU DON'T CONTROL.
*   **PROHIBITED:** Stating brands *will* give gear, cash, or gift cards.
*   **REQUIRED:** Proposal language only. "We'd like to explore a pilot pool," "If your team is open to co-designing a reward tier."

### 3. NO GUARANTEED OUTCOMES.
*   **PROHIBITED:** "Viral potential," "Guaranteed ROI," "Real-time metrics" (unless built).
*   **REQUIRED:** "Our goal is to measure," "We believe there is potential," "We want to test."

### 4. ALWAYS MARK ESTIMATES AS HYPOTHETICAL.
*   **REQUIRED:** Any sample data must be labeled: `(SAMPLE / HYPOTHETICAL ONLY ‚Äì not current data)`.

### 5. FRAMEWORK: "EARLY-STAGE PARTNERSHIP"
*   **TONE:** Humble, hungry, transparent founder.
*   **STANCE:** We are building this. We want you to be the first partners to shape it. We are not claiming to be Fortnite size yet.
