## The Problem

I've been gaming since I was 12. Valorant, CS:GO, League of Legends. Thousands of hours grinding ranked, climbing from Bronze to Diamond, perfecting my aim, learning every map.

And for what? A rank that resets every season. A badge that means nothing outside the game.

Meanwhile, I'm broke. Bootstrapping a startup, living on ramen, optimizing every dollar. My lola (grandmother) taught me: "Marunong magtipid" (know how to save). Work smart, not just hard.

I thought: **What if gamers could earn real rewards for the hours they're already putting in?**

Not crypto. Not NFTs. Not fake points that never convert to cash.

**Real rewards:** Gift cards. Merch. Coaching sessions. Stuff you can actually use.

---

## The Solution: GG Loop

**GG Loop** is a gaming rewards platform where you:
1. **Connect your gaming accounts** (Riot, Twitch, Steam)
2. **Play the games you already love** (Valorant, CS2, LoL, OW2)
3. **Earn points** based on performance (wins, kills, objectives)
4. **Redeem rewards** (gift cards, hoodies, coaching, charity donations)

No crypto. No NFTs. No fake urgency. Just honest rewards for honest gameplay.

## Why GG Loop Will Work

**1. Manual Fulfillment = Trust**  
We don't automate everything. Hoodies, coaching sessions, charity donationsâ€”these are manually processed. Slow, but builds trust. No bots, no scams.

**2. Transparent Pricing**  
No hidden fees. No surprise charges. You see exactly how many points you need and what you get.

**3. Community First**  
Discord is the heart of GG Loop. 2,000+ gamers helping each other rank up, sharing tips, celebrating wins.

**4. Honest Marketing**  
No fake urgency ("ONLY 2 LEFT!"). No overpromising ("GET RICH QUICK!"). Just real value for real gamers.

**5. Cultural Connection**  
Basketball grind = ranked grind. Same mentality: fundamentals, consistency, team-first. For the culture.
