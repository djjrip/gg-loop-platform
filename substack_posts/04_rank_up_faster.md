## What Actually Works

### 1. Fix Your Crosshair Placement (Not Aim Training)

**The Problem:**  
Plat players aim at the ground. Diamond players pre-aim head level.

**The Fix:**
- Keep crosshair at head level (always)
- Pre-aim common angles (learn the map)
- Practice in custom games, not deathmatch

**How to Practice:**
1. Load into a custom game (no bots)
2. Walk through the map
3. Keep crosshair at head level the entire time
4. Pre-aim every corner, every angle
5. Do this for 10 minutes before ranked

**Result:** You'll win more duels without "better aim."

---

### 2. Learn One Agent, Master It

**The Problem:**  
Plat players flex too much. They play 10 agents at a mediocre level instead of 1 agent at a high level.

**The Fix:**
- Pick ONE agent
- Play 100 games on that agent
- Learn every lineup, every trick, every matchup

**Why This Works:**
- You stop thinking about abilities
- You focus on fundamentals (positioning, crosshair, comms)
- You understand your role deeply

**My Pick:** Omen (controller, always needed, high impact)

**Your Pick:** Whatever you enjoy playing. Just pick ONE.

---

### 3. Economy Management

**The Problem:**  
Plat players force buy when they shouldn't. They don't coordinate with the team.

**The Fix:**
- **Full buy or save.** No half-buys.
- **Coordinate with team.** If 3 people are saving, you save too.
- **Don't force after losing pistol.** Save round 2, full buy round 3.

**Economy Rules:**
- Lost pistol? Save round 2.
- Lost 2 rounds in a row? Full save.
- Won a round? Full buy next round.
- Don't force unless it's match point.

**Result:** You'll have better guns more often. Better guns = more wins.

---

### 4. Comms That Matter

**The Problem:**  
Plat players either don't comm or comm too much (useless info, tilting, blaming).

**The Fix:**
- **Callouts only.** "One A site, low." That's it.
- **No tilting.** If you're tilting, mute yourself.
- **Mute toxic players instantly.** Don't argue. Just mute.

**Good Comms:**
- "One A site, low"
- "Sage wall down"
- "Rotating B"

**Bad Comms:**
- "Why didn't you trade me?"
- "This team is trash"
- "GG go next"

**Result:** Less tilt, better teamwork, more wins.

---

### 5. VOD Review (10 Minutes/Day)

**The Problem:**  
Plat players don't review their games. They repeat the same mistakes.

**The Fix:**
- Record one game per day (use Medal.tv or Outplayed)
- Watch your deaths (skip the rest)
- Identify patterns (dying to same angle, bad positioning, etc.)
- Fix ONE mistake at a time

**What to Look For:**
- Where did you die?
- Why did you die? (bad positioning, bad crosshair, bad timing)
- How can you avoid this next time?

**Result:** You stop repeating mistakes. You improve faster.
