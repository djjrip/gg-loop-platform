## SPONSORSHIP IDENTITY SYSTEM (VERIFIED)

**"Choose Your Sponsor" Feature:**
When players sign up, they pick a brand sponsor (like NIL deals for college athletes).

**How It Works:**
1. Player chooses brand (Nike, Adidas, Logitech, etc.)
2. Becomes "[Brand] Rookie" (player identity)
3. Earns brand-specific points
4. Unlocks exclusive brand rewards
5. Reps brand colors on profile
6. Joins brand-specific Discord channels

**Why This Works:**
- **Player Identity:** "I'm a Nike All-Star" > "I'm a user"
- **Brand Loyalty:** Creates emotional connection
- **Gamification:** Choosing sponsor is first quest
- **Revenue:** Brands pay for exclusive cohorts
- **Cultural Authenticity:** Mirrors real-world athlete sponsorships (NBA, NIL)

**Category Exclusivity Model:**
- One brand per category (Nike OR Adidas, not both)
- Cross-category freedom (Nike + Logitech + Cash App = allowed)
- Competition strengthens platform (Nike vs Adidas rivalry)

### Impact.com Integration Alignment

**Q: How do Impact.com affiliates fit with brand partnerships?**  
**A: COMPLEMENTARY.**
- Impact.com = affiliate revenue (GG LOOP earns commission)
- Brand partnerships = sponsored rewards (brands pay for cohorts)
- Both drive rewards for players
- Both provide revenue for GG LOOP
- No conflict (different models, different brands)

**Example:**
- Player is "Nike Rookie" (brand partnership)
- Player also earns GearUP Booster affiliate link (Impact.com)
- Player gets Nike rewards + GearUP commission
- GG LOOP gets brand partnership fee + affiliate commission
- Win-win-win

### PayPal Subscription Alignment

**A: SYNERGISTIC.**
