## üìù Typography

**Primary Font: Inter**
- **Weights Available:** 500 (Medium), 600 (Semibold), 700 (Bold)
- **Usage:** Body text, UI elements, general content
- **Download:** [Google Fonts - Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter)
- **License:** SIL Open Font License (Free for commercial use)

**Accent Font: Space Grotesk**
- **Weights Available:** 500-700
- **Usage:** Headlines, stats/numbers, bold statements, logos
- **Download:** [Google Fonts - Space Grotesk](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Space+Grotesk)
- **License:** SIL Open Font License (Free for commercial use)

---

### Typography Hierarchy (Print/Merch)

**T-Shirt/Apparel Headlines:**
- Font: Space Grotesk Bold (700)
- Size: 72-96pt minimum
- Tracking: Tight (-0.02em)
- Style: ALL CAPS or Title Case

**Taglines/Secondary Text:**
- Font: Inter Semibold (600)
- Size: 24-36pt
- Tracking: Normal
- Style: Sentence case

**Small Details/Labels:**
- Font: Inter Medium (500)
- Size: 10-14pt
- Tracking: Wide (0.05em)
- Style: UPPERCASE for labels

**Stats/Numbers (on merch):**
- Font: Space Grotesk Bold (700)
- Size: 48-120pt (make them BIG)
- Style: Standalone, feature numbers

## üé® Design Principles for Merchandise

### Overall Aesthetic
**"Premium Gaming Guild with Street Credibility"**

Think:
- ‚úÖ Nike SNKRS app (clean, premium, streetwear)
- ‚úÖ Apple product pages (sophisticated, minimal)
- ‚úÖ Premium esports team apparel (refined, not tacky)
- ‚ùå NOT: Loud RGB gamer aesthetic, casino vibes, excessive neon

---

### Visual Language

**Tone:** Sophisticated, grounded, earthy, exclusive
**Energy:** Tribal community, members-club, street credible
**Vibe:** Dark and warm (not cold tech), natural textures

**DO:**
- Use generous white space
- Embrace dark backgrounds with warm accents
- Keep designs minimal and refined
- Feature large bold typography
- Use subtle textures (concrete, clay, natural materials)
- Celebrate stats/numbers prominently
- Tribal/geometric patterns (minimal)

**DON'T:**
- No RGB rainbow chaos
- No excessive neon glows
- No casino/slot machine aesthetics
- No overly "gamer" stereotypes
- Avoid clutter and visual noise

## üìê Technical Specifications

### Print-Ready Files

**File Formats:**
- Vector: AI, EPS, SVG (preferred for logos/text)
- Raster: PNG (transparent), PSD (layered)
- Resolution: 300 DPI minimum for print

**Color Modes:**
- Print: CMYK color mode
- Digital: RGB color mode
- Pantone: 16-1440 TCX for exact terracotta matching

**Bleed:**
- Add 0.125" (3mm) bleed on all sides for print

---

### Embroidery Specifications

**Thread Colors:**
- Primary: Pantone 16-1440 TCX (terracotta)
- Black: True black (no charcoal)
- White: Bright white (for dark fabrics)

**Stitch Density:**
- Logo: 4-5mm satin stitch
- Text: 3-4mm for legibility
- Avoid fine details under 1mm
