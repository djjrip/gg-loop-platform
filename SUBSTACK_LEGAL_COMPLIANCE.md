### DISCLOSURE PLACEMENT RULES

**FTC Requirements:**
1. **Clear and Conspicuous:** Easy to see, read, and understand
2. **Prominent:** Large enough font, readable colors
3. **Before the Link:** Disclosure MUST appear before or at the affiliate link
4. **Simple Language:** "I earn a commission" not "material connection"
5. **Proximity:** Close to the affiliate link, not in footer

**GG Loop Standard Format:**
