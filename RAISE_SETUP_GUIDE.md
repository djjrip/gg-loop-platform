# Raise.com Quick Start Guide
**Get your account set up in 10 minutes**

## Step 1: Sign Up (2 minutes)

1. Go to **https://www.raise.com**
2. Click **"Sign Up"** (top right corner)
3. Enter:
   - Email: Use your business email (e.g., `admin@ggloop.io`)
   - Password: Strong password (save in password manager)
   - Name: Your name or business name
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. Check your email and click the verification link

## Step 2: Add Payment Method (3 minutes)

1. Click your profile icon (top right)
2. Go to **"Payment Methods"**
3. Click **"Add Payment Method"**
4. Choose your option:
   
   **Option A: Debit/Credit Card** (Recommended)
   - Instant purchases
   - Easy tracking
   - Works for all cards
   
   **Option B: PayPal**
   - Need to link PayPal account first
   - Slightly slower checkout
   - Good if you want buyer protection

5. Enter card details
6. Click **"Save"**

‚úÖ **Payment method added!**

## Troubleshooting

### "Card is out of stock"
- Try alternative platforms: CardCash, Cardpool
- Or wait 24-48 hours and check again
- Offer user alternative card of equal value

### "Code doesn't work"
- Contact Raise.com support immediately (live chat)
- They'll replace or refund within 24 hours
- Screenshot the error for proof

### "Payment declined"
- Check card balance / daily limit
- Try PayPal instead
- Contact your bank (might flag as unusual activity)
