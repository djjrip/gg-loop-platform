## TONE GUIDELINES

### DO:
- ✅ Be casual: "Link in bio if you want it"
- ✅ Be honest: "Yeah it's affiliate but it works"
- ✅ Be direct: "This is what I actually use"
- ✅ Be helpful: "Full guide in bio"
- ✅ Disclose: Always mention it's an affiliate link

### DON'T:
- ❌ Fake urgency: "ONLY 2 LEFT!!!" (unless true)
- ❌ Overpromise: "This will change your life!!!"
- ❌ Corporate speak: "Leverage this opportunity"
- ❌ Pushy: "You NEED to buy this NOW"
- ❌ Hide affiliate: Always disclose

## CTA TESTING FRAMEWORK

Test different CTAs to see what performs:

**Test 1: Direct vs Value-First**
- A: "Link in bio"
- B: "Full guide in bio (free + affiliate link)"

- A: "(Affiliate link)"
- B: "(Affiliate link - helps keep GG LOOP running)"
- Winner: Track conversions

**Test 3: Casual vs Honest**
- A: "Link in bio if you want it"
