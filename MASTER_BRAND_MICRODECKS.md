## 1. LOGITECH G DECK
*   **Slide 1: Title**
    *   **GG LOOP x Logitech G**
    *   Subtitle: "The Setup Grind" - A Pilot Proposal
*   **Slide 2: The User**
    *   Competitive Gamers (Valorant, League, Apex)
    *   Obsessed with Optimization
    *   Currently grinding for: Superlight Mice, Pro X Keyboards
*   **Slide 3: The Problem**
    *   Gamers play 1,000s of hours.
    *   They get "virtual" skins.
    *   They want *real* gear.
*   **Slide 4: The Solution**
    *   **Choose Your Sponsor:** A new onboarding flow.
    *   Player selects "Logitech G" as their sponsor.
    *   Player earns "Logitech Points" for positive play & wins.
*   **Slide 5: The Pilot**
    *   30-Day Test.
    *   Top performers earn tangible Logitech gear (provided by pilot pool).
    *   Zero downside risk.
*   **Slide 6: Success Metrics**
    *   Number of "Signings" (Brand affinity).
    *   Engagement retention vs. non-sponsored players.
