### Subject Lines
- **Format:** "Partnership Opportunity - GG LOOP Ã— [Brand]"
- **Alternative:** "[Brand] Gaming Partnership - GG LOOP"
- **Keep it:** Short, clear, professional

### Timing
- **Best Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best Times:** 9-11am or 2-4pm (their timezone)
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

### Follow-Up Schedule
- **Day 3:** Check if email was opened (if tracking)
- **Day 5:** Send follow-up email (short, polite)
- **Day 10:** Final follow-up, then move on

### Attachments
- **Pitch Deck:** Attach as PDF (customize per brand)
- **One-Pager:** Optional (if deck is too long)
- **Keep it:** Under 5MB total

## CONTACT RESEARCH TIPS

### LinkedIn
- Search: "[Brand] gaming partnerships"
- Search: "[Brand] digital marketing"
- Filter: Current employees only
- Look for: Director, Manager, Lead titles

### Twitter/X
- Follow brand's official account
- Check who they interact with
- Look for partnership announcements
- Engage before DMing

### Company Websites
- Check "Partnerships" or "Collaborate" pages
- Look for contact forms
- Review recent press releases
- Find team member bios
