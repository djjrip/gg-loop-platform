## TONE VARIATIONS

### Ultra Short (When to Use)
- **Best For:** Cold outreach, busy executives, quick intro
- **Tone:** Direct, no fluff, respectful of time
- **Goal:** Get a response, not close a deal

### Punchy (When to Use)
- **Best For:** Warm intros, referrals, follow-ups
- **Tone:** Energetic, clear value prop, confident
- **Goal:** Get a call scheduled

### Founder-to-Founder (When to Use)
- **Best For:** Reaching other founders, startup teams, cultural brands
- **Tone:** Peer-to-peer, collaborative, honest
- **Goal:** Build relationship, not just transactional

## PLATFORM-SPECIFIC TIPS

### LinkedIn
- **Best Time:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am or 2-4pm
- **Character Limit:** 300 characters for InMail subject + body
- **Response Rate:** ~10-20% for cold outreach
- **Tip:** Mention mutual connections if possible

### Twitter/X
- **Best Time:** Monday-Friday, 12-1pm or 5-6pm
- **Character Limit:** 280 characters per tweet
- **Response Rate:** ~5-10% for cold DMs
- **Tip:** Engage with their tweets first (like, retweet)

### Instagram
- **Best Time:** Daily, 6-9pm (when people scroll)
- **Character Limit:** No hard limit, but keep it short
- **Response Rate:** ~5-15% for cold DMs
- **Tip:** Comment on their posts first (build familiarity)
